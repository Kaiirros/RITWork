Berlin Fernsehturm (Television Tower)

Photo of the East Berlin TV Tower ("Fernsehturm")

Originally both a monument to the "greatness of communism" and a simple utility tower for television signals, the Berlin Fernsehturm is a must see attraction when in the city. The photos of the tower don't give it's size justice. At 368 meters or 1207 feet, this spectacle got even better when traveling to the top observation deck.

From the top, the view of the city could be seen 360 degrees around
