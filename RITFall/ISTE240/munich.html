City of Munich

The City of Munich is located in the southeast of the country in the state of Bavaria. The region is best known internationally from their traditions of wearing Lederhosens, and their strict "Reinheitsgebot", also known as their beer purity laws. Bavarians are also known for being the some of the world's most copious beer drinkers, with the statistics of 170 liters per person, anually.

The capital city of Munich was a great place to visit during my trip. When we visited, it was during the city's 865th birthday, which made the streets very lively at night.
