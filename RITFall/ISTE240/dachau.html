Inside the Dachau Concentration Camp

The trip to Dachau, just north of Munich, was an eye-opening moment. Seeing the former location of a Nazi Germany concentration camp as well as rebuilt features for the museum allowed for one to see some of the horrific natured areas that the camp used to utilize. The location had incredible information regarding other concentration camps throughout Germany, and the history shown was both disturbing and insightful.

Photo of the outer fence of Dachau

Being able to see the inside of a Nazi Concentration Camp is important for those who wish to gain more knowledge on the suffering that occurred throughout Europe. It is truely an eye-opener.
