Neuschwanstein Castle

Front of the Neuschwanstein Castle

Comissioned to be built by King Ludwig the Second of Bavaria (also known as the Mad King Ludwig) in 1869, Neuschwanstein Castle, meaning New-Swan-Rock Castle, was an incredibly beautiful castle in southern Bavaria. The castle was never officially finished, but tours are allowed inside, barring any sort of photography. This part of the trip will always be the most fascinating. It's almost fantasy-like, walking around the grounds of a disney-like castle. I highly recommend visiting Neuschwanstein if you are in the Bavarian State.
