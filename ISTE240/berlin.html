City of Berlin

Historically, the old capital of Prussia, currently, the capital of Germanyâ€”Berlin is the most populated city in the country and is especially diverse. It's history is especially rich, with it being divided after World War 2 between the allied powers, which created the West and East German states.

West Berlin

In the historical region of West Berlin, you can find landmarks like the impressive Victory Column and beautiful, massive Tiergarten (Park that is literally translated to "Animal Garden"), and wonderful streetway, Unter den Linden ("Under the Lindon"), known for it's rows of Linden trees.

East Berlin

On the communist controlled side of Berlin, you can find landmarks like the Berlin Television Tower, the famous Museum island containing 5 different museums, and the East Side Gallery, a stretch of the Berlin wall that is still intact today.
