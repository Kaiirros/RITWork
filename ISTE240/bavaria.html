State of Bavaria

The German State of Bavaria is located in the southeast of the country. It has many notable cities including the capital, Munich. It's best known internationally from their traditions of wearing Lederhosens, and their strict "Reinheitsgebot," also known as their beer purity laws. Bavarians are also known for being the some of the world's most copious beer drinkers, with the statistics of 170 liters per person, anually.

Munich
