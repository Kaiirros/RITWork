The Brandenburg Gate

Brandenburg Gate during popular hours

Featured on the German 50 euro cent coin, the Brandenburg Gate is a staple of German & Prussian culture. The Quadriga, the four horse chariot formerly ridden by Eirene, the goddess of peace was donned on the top of the gate. During the Napoleonic Wars, Napoleon captured the statue and brought it back to Paris. Eventually, during the Prussian occupation of Paris, the statue was changed from Eirene to Victoria, the goddess of Victory.
