Frankfurt

At first glance, Frankfurt may seem like the perfect blend of modern and old, historic landmarks. This may look true, but during World War 2, almost all of Frankfurt's historic buildings were destroying in bombing raids. Sometime during the 50s, the Allied Powers who controlled West Germany, helped the city rebuild it's lost historic buildings.

Being the first city in Germany that I visited, it was a great starting experience, with great landmarks and good food. While walking, there was always something to look at.
