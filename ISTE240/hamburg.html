City of Hamburg

Hamburg is the second largest city in Germany. It's widely known for it's port, where thousands of ships pass through weekly. The world rewnowned band the Beatles was also known to have a long period of residence in Hamburg to work on their music. It's also the headquarters for the plane manufacturer Airbus.

Hamburg was a fascinating stop, and seeing the city as well as the port was a great addition to my trip.
